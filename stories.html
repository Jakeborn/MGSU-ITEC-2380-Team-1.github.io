Veteran Stories

Real voices from those whose lives were changed by service animals.

"Before Max, I barely left the house. Now, I go to the grocery store without fear. He's more than a dog — he's my freedom."

Sergeant Marcus Taylor, USMC (Ret.)

Marcus served two tours overseas and struggled with PTSD after returning home. When he was paired with Max, a Labrador trained in anxiety interruption, his life began to turn around. Max senses Marcus’ anxiety before it escalates, grounding him with touch and presence.

"Luna doesn't just guide me — she gives me the confidence to live fully again."

Corporal Jennifer Lee, Army Veteran

After losing her vision due to combat injuries, Jennifer felt isolated. Luna, a German Shepherd trained in mobility and visual guidance, brought purpose and independence back into Jennifer's daily life. Their bond is unbreakable.
